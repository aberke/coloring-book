The rotations we have been finding for shapes are symmetries of these shapes - they are transformations that leave the shapes unchanged.

When shapes have the same symmetries, they share a symmetry group.

We can call the group with 2 rotations C2, and call the the group with 3 rotations C3, call the group with 4 rotations C4, and so on...
