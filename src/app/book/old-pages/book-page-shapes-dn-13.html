You may have also noticed that our rotations can be reversed as well.


When a square is rotated by a 1/4 turn, rotating again by a 3/4 turn brings it back to the position it started in. The result is the same as a 0 turn.

1/4 turn ✷ 3/4 turn = 0 turn

The same can be said the other way around.

3/4 turn ✷ 1/4 turn = 0 turn
