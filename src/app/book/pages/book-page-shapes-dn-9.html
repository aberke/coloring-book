Again, color can reduce the amount of symmetry a shape has.

➞

For example, a D6 shape has 6 mirrors and 6 rotations, but color can transform it into a shape with only 3 mirrors and 3 rotations - a D3 shape.

➞

Color can reduce a D6 shape to a D3 shape because D3 is a subgroup of D6. Similarly, D4 is a subgroup of D8.
