We already saw how shapes that share the same set of symmetries share a symmetry group, but then we only considered rotations. Symmetry groups can have rotations as well as reflections.

We’ll call the symmetry group of a regular triangle, and all the shapes that have its same 3 rotations and 3 reflections, D3. And we’ll call the symmetry group with the 4 rotations and 4 reflections of a square D4, while we call the symmetry group with 5 rotations and 5 reflections D5, and so on...
