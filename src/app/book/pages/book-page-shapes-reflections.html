Even when two shapes have the same number of rotations, one can still have more symmetry than the other.

Some shapes have mirrors - they can reflect across internal, invisible lines without changing in appearance.

While others cannot.

These mirrors are symmetries of our shapes, and we’ll see how they can be removed by adding color.


First, let’s generate more mirrors.
