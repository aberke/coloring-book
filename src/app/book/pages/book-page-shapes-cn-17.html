Not all sets of rotations are groups, and therefore cannot be subgroups. Try to color a shape in a way so that it has only a 0 turn and a 1/4 turn.

It's impossible without also giving the shape a 2/4 turn and a 3/4 turn. That's because {0 turn, 1/4 turn } is not a group, but {0 turn, 1/4 turn, 2/4 turn, 3/4 turn } is.
