The triangle has only 3 unique rotations. We'll talk about rotations that are less than a full turn.

Other shapes have these same 3 rotations. For this reason, we can say they all share the same symmetry group.


However, their rotational symmetry can be removed by adding color.

Now when our shape is rotated, its color shows it.
