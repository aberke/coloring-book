When one transformation, like a 1/4 turn, reverses the work of another transformation, it's called an inverse.



The 1/4 turn is the inverse of the 3/4 turn in C4, and vise-versa.

Similarly, the 1/3 turn and 2/3 turn are inverses in C3.

C3: 1/3 turn ✷ 2/3 turn = 0 turn = 2/3 turn ✷ 1/3 turn
