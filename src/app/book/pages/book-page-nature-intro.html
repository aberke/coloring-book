Shapes & Symmetries



Symmetry presents itself in nature, but often with imperfections.

Math creates a space where perfect symmetry can be explored.

In our real physical world, lines may not be perfectly straight, and squares may not be perfectly square, but mathematics allows us to believe in straight lines and perfect squares.

Throughout this book, we will pretend we are in that mathematical space. We will ignore the imperfections in our drawings, and see shapes and patterns as if they are composed of perfect lines and curves.

We will use color to play with symmetry, and even destroy it at times, all in order to better understand it.
