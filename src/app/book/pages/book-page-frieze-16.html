The 7 Frieze Groups may yield an infinite number of patterns, but this infinity is small compared to the number of patterns of the Wallpaper Groups.


Frieze patterns are limited to repetition in one direction, but Wallpaper patterns do not have that limit.



When that limit is removed for the Wallpaper Groups, the number of possible patterns and amount of symmetry within them grows beyond what we have colored.



Challenge: Can you find all of the directions in which the pattern repeats?
