C3:        1/3 turn ✷ 1/3 turn ➞ 2/3 turn


Adding another 1/3 turn brings the shape back to its starting position - the 0 turn.

C3:         1/3 turn ✷ 1/3 turn ✷ 1/3 turn ➞ 0 turn
