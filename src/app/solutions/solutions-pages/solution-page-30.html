SHAPES & SYMMETRIES: ROTATIONS: COLORING & CHALLENGES 30

Use color to reduce the C12 shape to a C6 shape. Is it possible to add more color to reduce it to a C4 shape? What about a C3 shape?

Answer: color can further reduce the C6 shape to a C3 shape, but not a C4 shape. C3 is a subgroup of both C12 and C6, but C4 is not a subgroup of C6.
