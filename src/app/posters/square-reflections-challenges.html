If you enjoyed these puzzles, then you'll likely enjoy Beautiful Symmetry: A Coloring Book About Math, authored by Alex Berke, with a forward by Alex Bellos. In this "book" there are visual puzzles and coloring challenges, somewhat like the puzzles above, with shapes

as well as infinitely repeating patterns.

It is also a gentle introduction to the mathematics of symmetry.

The book is published by the MIT Press and is available in print via all major booksellers: https://mitpress.mit.edu/books/beautiful-symmetry. There is also a (free) online version of the book that brings the symmetries of its illustrations to life with interactive animations: http://beautifulsymmetry.onl.
